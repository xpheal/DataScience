Did You Know?

Trivia

The film's wide release date was originally moved from November 25th to August 5th in 2016. The Weinstein Company said publicly that the film was a strong Academy Awards contender, and they wanted to show that a film like that could do well during the summer release season instead of simply positioning it in the late fall/winter area when most films that are considered "Oscar contenders" get "platform" releases that go to larger numbers of screens near the end of one year or at the very start of the next one. However, in July TWC decided to shift the film back into the "Oscar contenders" release path, and it will now open in select cities in December 2016 before getting a wide release in early January 2017.
